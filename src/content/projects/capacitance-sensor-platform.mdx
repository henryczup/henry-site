A low-cost capacitive sensing system for wearable applications that can be scanned and read with a smartphone.

## Highlights
- 75–90% cost reduction vs Bluetooth systems
- Estimated $0.40–$0.65 per unit at scale
- 2nd place ECE Undergraduate Research Symposium (patent filed)
