## What it does
A low-cost capacitive sensing system for wearable applications that can be scanned and read with a smartphone. The system pairs printed or fabricated capacitive sensors with a cross-platform mobile app for real-time scanning, visualization, and logging.

## Highlights
- Built an iOS and Android app to scan, visualize, and log capacitance data in real time
- Reduced estimated cost by 75-90% compared with Bluetooth systems
- Estimated $0.40-$0.65 per unit at scale
- Awarded 2nd place among 37 projects at the ECE Undergraduate Research Symposium
- Patent filed
