## What it is
A Python workflow for inverse design of RF/microwave and antenna structures. The workflow combines CST simulation, optimization loops, surrogate models, and physics-based models to predict and improve multiport electromagnetic performance.

## Highlights
- Automated CST simulation workflows for multiport design exploration
- Built fast surrogate and physics-based models for EM performance prediction
- Applied the framework to antennas, matching networks, and Doherty-combiner design
- Focused on reducing manual iteration in RF design workflows
