## What it does
A real-time computer vision system that analyzes running form, surfaces biomechanical metrics, and helps runners identify technique issues that can affect efficiency or injury risk.

## Highlights
- Extracts 20+ biomechanical metrics at 35 FPS
- Designed for live feedback rather than offline-only analysis
- Tracks form signals relevant to technique, symmetry, and movement quality
